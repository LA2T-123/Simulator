Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

Our Bitcoin Mining Simulator is an educational tool that operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect or store any personal information. The simulator:

  • Runs locally in your browser
  • Does not transmit any data to our servers
  • Does not store any information about your mining sessions

2. Browser Storage

We use local browser storage to:

  • Save your mining preferences
  • Maintain your session statistics
  • Improve your user experience

This data is stored only on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Chart.js for data visualization
  • Font Awesome for icons
  • Google Fonts for typography

These services may collect usage data according to their respective privacy policies.

4. Cookies

Our simulator does not use cookies. Any data storage is handled through local browser storage only.

5. Children's Privacy

Our simulator is suitable for educational purposes and can be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@bitcoinminingsimulator.com
